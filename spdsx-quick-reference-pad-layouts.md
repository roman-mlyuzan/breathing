## Kit Switching Cheat Sheet

### Performance Timeline

```
Time  | From → To      | Method         | Notes
------|----------------|----------------|------------------------
0:00  | START: Kit 2   | —              | Opening calm
5:00  | 2(48) → 3(60)  | Texture Fade   | Gentle start
6:30  | 3(60) → 4(72)  | On-the-One     | Building begins
8:30  | 4(72) → 5(90)  | Fill Switch    | Energy rises
10:00 | 5(90) → 6(100) | On-the-One     | Steady build
11:30 | 6(100) → 7(120)| Fill Switch    | ⭐ BIG MOMENT
13:00 | 7(120) → 3(60) | Texture Fade   | ⚠️ RAPID DROP
15:00 | 3(60) → 2(48)  | Texture Fade   | Deep calm
------|----------------|----------------|------------------------
21:00 | 2(48) → 4(72)  | On-the-One     | Wave 2 start
23:00 | 4(72) → 5(90)  | Fill Switch    | Fast build
25:00 | 5(90) → 7(120) | On-the-One     | Skip Kit 6!
27:00 | 7(120) → 8(128)| Fill Switch    | ⭐⭐ PEAK!
31:00 | 8(128) → 4(72) | Texture Fade   | ⚠️ Landing
33:00 | 4(72) → 3(60)  | On-the-One     | Continuing down
35:00 | 3(60) → 2(48)  | Texture Fade   | Grounding
37:00 | 2(48) → 1(44)  | Texture Fade   | Final silence
40:00 | SILENCE        | —              | Hold 2-3 min
```

### Switch Methods Explained

**On-the-One:** Instant switch on beat 1 of new phrase (seamless)
**Fill Switch:** Play fill, switch on last beat, new kit on downbeat
**Texture Fade:** Reduce to pads only, switch during sustain, rebuild

## Emergency Quick Reference

### If You Lose Tempo

**Calm Phase:** Lock to breathing you HEAR (watch chests)
**Energetic Phase:** Lock to bass player OR check click

### If Participants Hyperventilate

1. **Immediately slow 10-15 BPM**
2. Strip to kick + cymbal only
3. Create grounding pulse
4. Wait for facilitator verbal cues

### If Kit Won't Switch

- Keep playing current kit
- Signal band "hold steady"
- Try switch again on next phrase
- Worst case: stay on current kit and adjust

## Most Important Things to Remember

1. **Kit 7 (120 BPM) is your weapon** - Lock it steady, hold it 2+ min
2. **Pad 1 = KICK on every kit** - Muscle memory
3. **Calm kits (1-3): Space is sacred** - Less is more
4. **Energetic kits (5-8): Precision matters** - Don't rush
5. **Transitions at 13:00 and 31:00 are hardest** - Mark them clearly
6. **Trust the click track at 120 BPM** - Your feel will rush
7. **Honor the silence at 40:00** - Hold 2-3 minutes minimum
